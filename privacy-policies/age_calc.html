Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Age Calculator app ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Our app collects and stores the following personal information:

  • Birth Date: We store your birth date locally on your device to calculate your age information. This data is only stored on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • App Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics through Firebase Analytics to improve our app's performance and user experience.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App performance data and crash reports
  • Usage patterns and features accessed
  • Advertising identifiers for personalized ads

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: Calculate and display your age information in various formats (Korean age, international age, etc.)
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Personalized Experience: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Advertising: Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
Important: Your birth date is stored exclusively on your device. We never transmit, store, or have access to this personal information on our servers.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We work with the following third-party services:

3.1 Google Firebase Analytics

  • Collects anonymous usage statistics and crash reports
  • Helps us understand how users interact with our app
  • Data is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy

3.2 Google AdMob

  • Displays advertisements within the app
  • May use advertising identifiers to show personalized ads
  • Users can opt out of personalized ads in their device settings

3.3 Data Not Shared

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your birth date with any external services
  • Use your data for purposes other than those stated in this policy

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: Your birth date is encrypted and stored securely on your device using industry-standard practices
  • Data Transmission: All communications with our servers use encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access Controls: We limit access to user data to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security practices and dependencies

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view your stored birth date within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your birth date through the app settings or by uninstalling the app
  • Correction: You can update your birth date at any time within the app

5.2 Advertising Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's privacy settings
  • Visit Google Ad Settings to manage your ad preferences
  • Use your device's "Limit Ad Tracking" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" feature

5.3 Analytics Opt-out

While we don't collect personally identifiable information through analytics, you can limit data collection by:

  • Using your device's privacy settings to limit app analytics
  • Contacting us to request data deletion

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be family-friendly and can be used by people of all ages. However:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents can contact us to review, delete, or stop further collection of their child's information

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including:

  • Google Services: Data processed by Firebase and AdMob may be transferred to Google's global infrastructure
  • Data Protection: All transfers comply with applicable privacy laws and Google's privacy standards
