PRIVACY POLICY

Daily Habit Tracker

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Daily Habit Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

CONTACT INFORMATION
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: contact@jobby-time.com

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information
We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The app operates locally on your device.

Usage Data
We may collect information about how the app is accessed and used. This may include:
- App usage statistics
- Feature interaction data
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- Crash reports and performance data

Local Data Storage
All your habit data, including:
- Habit names and descriptions
- Check-in records
- Progress statistics
- Custom settings

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app functionality
- Improve and optimize app performance
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob

3. ADVERTISING

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for ad personalization. This includes:
- Device advertising ID
- IP address
- General location data
- Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

For more information about Google's privacy practices, visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- All habit data is stored locally on your device
- We do not transmit personal data to external servers
- We use industry-standard security practices for any analytics data collected

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. DATA RETENTION

Local Data:
- Habit data remains on your device until you delete the app
- You can delete individual habits or clear all data through the app settings

Analytics Data:
- Anonymous usage statistics are retained for up to 24 months
- Crash reports are retained for 90 days

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at contact@jobby-time.com.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access your habit data (available in the app)
- Delete your data (through app settings or by uninstalling)
- Opt out of personalized advertising
- Request information about data practices by contacting us

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):
Under GDPR, you have additional rights including the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For California residents:
Under CCPA, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion of your information, and opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).
